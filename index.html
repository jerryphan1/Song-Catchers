ABOUT

As music continues to grow and expand, so does the desire to learn the lyrics. Whether it be a new radio hit, or that one song you heard long ago, we all want to impress our friends whenever we have that spontaneous karaoke night. Song Catchers is a easy to use website that gives you everything you need for such success.

Features of this website include a search function to find any song or artist and being able to view the corresponding lyrics. Users will also be able to see the artist of the specified song alongside his or her top hits, as well as similar artists. Upon clicking on a top hit or similar artist, users will be directed to the Spotify app for viewing.

To learn more reach out to me via the icons provided under the links section. Thank you for checking out the website, enjoy!
